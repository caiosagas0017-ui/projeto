Dental Services

We offer a variety of services to our patients with cutting-edge technology and maximum comfort.

White Fillings

Composite resin restorations that match the natural color of your teeth, eliminating the metallic look for a natural appearance and long-lasting durability.

Root Canal Treatment

Endodontic therapy using new rotary instrumentation for faster, safer, and more comfortable results, saving damaged teeth and relieving pain.

Dental Bonding

Application of durable resin to close spaces, correct misaligned teeth, and mask unwanted stains quickly and affordably.

Porcelain Veneers

Thin, durable shells that correct stained or damaged teeth. Translucent and stain-resistant for an incredibly natural and realistic smile.

Lumineers

Veneers as thin as contact lenses. A painless, permanent solution to whiten and perfectly align smiles with little to no tooth reduction.

Dental Crowns

Restore the structural integrity of damaged teeth, allowing normal function. Custom-made from metal, porcelain, or a combination of both.

In-Office Whitening

Treatment activated by the Rembrandt Sapphire light that can lighten your teeth an average of eight shades in just one hour. Safe, easy, and convenient.

At-Home Whitening

For those who prefer flexibility and convenience, we offer a variety of at-home whitening products to achieve a sparkling smile on your own schedule.

Dental Implants

A state-of-the-art treatment for missing teeth. A permanent titanium root with a custom prosthetic crown that looks and functions like a natural tooth.

Dental Bridges

Fill the gaps left by missing teeth by anchoring to adjacent teeth. They prevent other teeth from drifting and maintain the health and beauty of your smile.

Partial / Full Dentures

Replacement of all or a few missing teeth with removable prosthetics. Today's dentures are more lifelike than ever, restoring function and appearance.

Our Dentists

Brian D. Tucker, DMD, FAGD

Lead Dentist & Owner

Dr. Brian D. Tucker was born and raised in Cinnaminson, New Jersey. He received his Bachelor of Science in Biology at The College of New Jersey. He continued his studies at the University of Medicine and Dentistry of New Jersey, where he earned his dental degree. He was selected for the prestigious Omicron Kappa Upsilon (OKU) Alumni Association.

During his residency in Las Vegas, Dr. Tucker gained valuable clinical experience in all aspects of dentistry, including implants, IV sedation, cosmetic dentistry, and complex extractions. He moved to Austin in 2006.

Dr. Tucker achieved the coveted Fellowship of the Academy of General Dentistry (FAGD) title in 2009, a distinction earned by only 6% of dentists in the US and Canada, reinforcing his ongoing commitment to excellence.

Sara Kieschnick, DDS

Dentist

Dr. Sara Kieschnick was born in Winnipeg, Manitoba, and grew up in Fairfax, Virginia, and Plano, Texas. She earned her bachelor's degree, graduating summa cum laude, from Austin College. She completed her Doctor of Dental Surgery (DDS) at the Texas A&M Baylor College of Dentistry in 2016.

After graduation, she completed a general practice residency in Albuquerque, New Mexico. Dr. Kieschnick regularly participates in continuing education programs, attending conferences and study groups to stay up-to-date with the latest techniques.

When not working, she enjoys spending time with her husband and young daughter, reading her favorite library books, and hanging out with friends.

Priscilla Wong, DDS

Dentist

Dr. Priscilla Wong was born near the Canadian Rockies in Calgary, Alberta. Her family moved to Austin, Texas, in 2001, and she has been a Texan at heart ever since.

She graduated in Chemical Engineering from the University of Texas at Austin and completed her Doctor of Dental Surgery at the University of Texas Health Houston School of Dentistry.

In her free time, she explores Austin's food scene with her husband, enjoys the outdoors, and is an excellent baker (specializing in brown butter chocolate chip cookies). She speaks fluent Cantonese and is currently learning Mandarin.
